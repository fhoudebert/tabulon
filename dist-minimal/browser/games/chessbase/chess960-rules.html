Randomized Initial Setup

Chess 960 is played with Orthodox Chess rules, but the starting position is randomized for all pieces except pawns.

Randomization restrictions:

  • The 2 Rooks can't be on the same side of the King
  • The 2 Bishops must stand on different colors
  • The final board must be symetrical (black and white diagrams are mirror images)

Casting is possible and always leads to Orthodox castling final positions, no matter the starting position.
