Differences from orthodox chess

Played on a 12x8 board, 3 fairy pieces:

  • The Archer (aka elephant) : can jump

  • The Champion (aka warrior) : can jump

  • The Paladin (aka centaur) : can jump

Inital setup

Each player starts with:

  • 12 Pawns
  • 2 Rooks
  • 2 Knights
  • 2 Archers
  • 2 Bishops
  • 1 Paladin
  • 1 Champion
  • 1 Queen
  • 1 king

Castling: yes.

"En passant" capture: yes.

Double move for Pawns (start): yes.
